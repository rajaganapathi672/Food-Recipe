Elaneer Payasam (Tender Coconut Kheer)

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Tender coconut water
  • ½ cup Tender coconut pulp (scooped from elaneer)
  • 1½ cups Boiled & chilled milk (full cream preferred)
  • ¼ cup Condensed milk (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tsp Sugar (optional, adjust if using more condensed milk)
  • ¼ tsp Cardamom powder (optional)
  • Chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) – for garnish
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil and cool the milk completely. Keep it chilled.
  2. In a blender, add coconut pulp, condensed milk, and a little tender coconut water. Blend coarsely.
  3. Mix the blended coconut mixture with chilled milk and remaining tender coconut water.
  4. Add sugar if needed. Add cardamom powder if using.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  6. Serve chilled, garnished with nuts and saffron strands.

Note: Do not boil or heat after adding tender coconut. Serve fresh and chilled only.
