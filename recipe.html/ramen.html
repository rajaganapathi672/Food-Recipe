Ichiraku Ramen (Anime Inspired)

Ingredients:

  • Ramen noodles - 2 packs
  • Chicken broth - 4 cups
  • Boiled egg - 2 halves
  • Soy sauce - 2 tbsp
  • Spring onions, chopped
  • Narutomaki (optional, for authentic look)
  • Sliced pork or chicken (cooked)
  • Salt, pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil chicken broth with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook ramen noodles as per instructions.
  3. Place noodles in a bowl and pour hot broth over it.
  4. Top with boiled egg, meat, narutomaki, and spring onions.
  5. Serve hot just like Naruto would eat!
