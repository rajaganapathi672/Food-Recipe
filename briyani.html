Mutton Biryani

Prep Time: 1 hour

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 5

Ingredients:

  • 500g Mutton (bone-in)
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup curd
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Mint and coriander leaves – handful each
  • Salt and oil as needed

Instructions:

  1. Marinate mutton with curd, chili powder, turmeric, salt, and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  2. Pressure cook mutton for 3–4 whistles or until tender.
  3. In a pot, heat oil and fry onions till golden brown. Set half aside for garnish.
  4. Add whole spices, tomatoes, mint, and coriander. Sauté well.
  5. Add cooked mutton with its stock. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Cook rice separately till 70% done. Strain and keep aside.
  7. Layer rice over mutton mixture. Add fried onions and mint on top.
  8. Cover with lid and dum cook on low flame for 15–20 minutes.
  9. Gently fluff and serve hot with raita or brinjal gravy.
