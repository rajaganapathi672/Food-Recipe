Arisi Paruppu Sadham

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Raw rice
  • ½ cup Toor dal (thuvaram paruppu)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 2–3 Garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 2 Dried red chilies
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp Cumin seeds
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Sambar powder (or chili powder)
  • Few Curry leaves
  • 2 tsp Oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water – as needed (about 3 cups)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice and toor dal together for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker. Add mustard seeds, cumin, and let them splutter.
  3. Add dried chilies, curry leaves, garlic, onion – sauté till translucent.
  4. Add tomato, turmeric, sambar powder and salt. Cook till mushy.
  5. Add soaked rice and dal. Mix well.
  6. Pour about 3 cups water. Pressure cook for 3–4 whistles.
  7. Let pressure release naturally. Gently mix and mash slightly.
  8. Drizzle ghee on top. Serve hot with appalam or pickle.
