Chapathi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Whole wheat flour (atta)
  • ¾ cup Water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tsp Oil (optional)
  • Salt – a pinch (optional)
  • Extra dry flour – for rolling
  • Ghee or butter – for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix wheat flour with salt (optional).
  2. Slowly add water and knead into a soft, smooth dough (not sticky).
  3. Cover and let the dough rest for 20–30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls.
  5. Roll each ball into a thin round chapathi using dry flour as needed.
  6. Heat a tawa or skillet. Place the chapathi on it and cook for a few seconds.
  7. Flip when small bubbles appear. Cook both sides evenly, pressing gently for puffing.
  8. Apply ghee if desired. Serve hot with kurma, chutney, or sabzi.
