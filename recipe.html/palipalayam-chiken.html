Pallipalayam Chicken

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (with bone, small pieces)
  • 15–20 Dry red chilies (adjust spice)
  • 1 cup Shallots (sambar onion), sliced
  • 2 tbsp Garlic (sliced or crushed)
  • 2 tbsp Coconut bits (small pieces)
  • 3 tbsp Gingelly oil (nalla ennai)
  • Few Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat gingelly oil in a kadai.
  2. Add dry red chilies, curry leaves, and coconut pieces. Sauté until slightly crisp.
  3. Add shallots and garlic. Fry till they turn golden brown.
  4. Add cleaned chicken pieces and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cook on medium flame till chicken releases water.
  6. Cover and cook until chicken is soft and cooked through (about 20–25 minutes).
  7. Once cooked, open the lid and fry till oil separates and chicken turns dry and roasted.
  8. Serve hot with rasam rice, chapati, or parotta.
