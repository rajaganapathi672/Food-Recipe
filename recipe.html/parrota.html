parrota

Prep Time: 40 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Maida (All-purpose flour)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil (for cooking)

Instructions:

  1. Mix maida, salt, oil and enough water to form a soft dough.
  2. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into balls, roll and stretch them, coil and flatten again.
  4. Cook each parotta on hot tawa with ghee/oil till golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with salna or kurma.
